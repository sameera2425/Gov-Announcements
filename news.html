New Education Scholarship for Rural Students
Ministry of Education | May 15, 2025 | 3 min read
Listen to this announcement
Students from rural areas can now apply for a new scholarship program that covers tuition fees and provides a monthly stipend. Applications are open until June 30, 2025. The initiative aims to support underprivileged students and promote higher education in rural India.

The scholarship program, launched under the National Education Policy 2020, will provide comprehensive financial support to meritorious students from rural backgrounds. The program includes:

• Full coverage of tuition fees for undergraduate and postgraduate courses
• Monthly stipend of ₹5,000 for living expenses
• Additional ₹2,000 per month for study materials
• One-time laptop allowance of ₹25,000
• Travel allowance for attending interviews and examinations

The selection process will be based on academic performance, family income, and a personal interview. Special consideration will be given to students from economically weaker sections and those belonging to scheduled castes and tribes.
Eligibility Criteria:
• Must be a resident of rural areas as defined by the 2011 Census
• Family income should not exceed ₹3,00,000 per annum
• Minimum 75% marks in previous qualifying examination
• Age limit: 17-25 years for undergraduate courses, 22-28 years for postgraduate courses

Required Documents:
• Aadhaar Card
• Income Certificate
• Domicile Certificate
• Previous Academic Records
• Bank Account Details
• Passport Size Photographs

How to Apply:
1. Visit the official government portal (scholarship.gov.in)
2. Register with your Aadhaar number
3. Fill out the online application form
4. Upload scanned copies of required documents
5. Submit the application before the deadline

Important Dates:
• Application Start Date: May 15, 2025
• Application Deadline: June 30, 2025
• Document Verification: July 1-15, 2025
• Interview Schedule: July 20-25, 2025
• Final Selection List: August 1, 2025

Contact Information:
For more information, contact your district education office or:
• Helpline: 1800-XXX-XXXX (Toll Free)
• Email: rural.scholarship@education.gov.in
• Office Hours: Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM
