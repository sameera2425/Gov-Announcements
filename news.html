New Education Scholarship for Rural Students
Ministry of Education  |  May 15, 2025 3 min read
Students from rural areas can now apply for a new scholarship program that covers tuition fees and provides a monthly stipend. Applications are open until June 30, 2025. The initiative aims to support underprivileged students and promote higher education in rural India.
Listen to this announcement
Eligibility: Students from rural backgrounds currently enrolled in recognized schools.
How to Apply: Visit the official government portal and fill out the online application form.
Contact: For more information, contact your district education office.
